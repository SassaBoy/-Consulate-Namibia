Welcome

Welcome to the Honorary Consulate

Welcome to the official website of the Honorary Consulate of Namibia to the Republic of Equatorial Guinea. The Consulate is dedicated to advancing and strengthening the longstanding relations between the two nations. Through diplomatic engagement, economic cooperation, and people-to-people partnerships, the Consulate serves as an important platform for fostering mutual understanding, trade, investment, tourism, and cultural exchange. Under the leadership of Honorary Consul Dr. Rolland Faouzi Salou, the Consulate promotes Namibia's national interests while facilitating constructive bilateral cooperation in key sectors including energy, mining, infrastructure, agriculture, education, innovation, tourism, and sustainable development.

Our Mandate & Role

2023Established
6+Sectors Covered
2Nations Served
100%Committed

The Honorary Consulate of Namibia to Equatorial Guinea represents the interests of the Republic of Namibia and contributes to strengthening diplomatic, economic, and cultural relations between the two countries. The Consulate is mandated to facilitate bilateral cooperation and promote constructive engagement between public institutions, private sector stakeholders, investors, development partners, and civil society organizations. It also supports initiatives aimed at enhancing regional integration, trade promotion, investment facilitation, tourism development, and institutional collaboration.

Honorary Consul

Dr. Rolland Faouzi Salou

Dr. Rolland Faouzi Salou is an accomplished international business executive and diplomat with extensive experience in investment promotion, regional cooperation, strategic partnerships, and economic development initiatives across the African continent. In his capacity as Honorary Consul, Dr. Salou actively promotes Namibia's economic and developmental priorities by facilitating bilateral cooperation, encouraging foreign direct investment, and supporting strategic partnerships in sectors of mutual interest.

His distinguished career spans multiple sectors across Sub-Saharan Africa, bringing a unique combination of business acumen and diplomatic expertise to the role. Dr. Salou is dedicated to building bridges between the business communities of Namibia and Equatorial Guinea, creating pathways for sustained economic collaboration and people-to-people partnerships.

Consular Services

How We Can Assist You

The Consulate provides a range of services to Namibian citizens, investors, and bilateral stakeholders.

Travel Documents

Passport guidance and travel documentation support for Namibian nationals residing in or visiting Equatorial Guinea.

Emergency Assistance

Urgent support for Namibian citizens experiencing distress or emergency situations in Equatorial Guinea.

Document Certification

Authentication and certification of official documents, including notarial acts and apostilles for use in Namibia.

Civil Registration

Assistance with registration of births, marriages, and deaths occurring abroad for Namibian nationals.

Business Missions

Coordinated support for official delegations, trade missions, and high-level business visits between the two nations.

Institutional Liaison

Facilitation of communication and engagement with Namibian government authorities and public institutions.

The Consular Section operates Monday to Friday, 08:00–17:00, excluding public holidays in Namibia and Equatorial Guinea.

A Strategic Destination for Investment

Namibia is widely recognised as one of Africa's most stable, transparent, and investment-oriented economies. Strategically located along the southwestern coast of Africa, Namibia serves as a gateway to regional and international markets, offering world-class infrastructure, abundant natural resources, and a robust legal framework for investors.
